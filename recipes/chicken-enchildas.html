Green Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 can (4 oz) diced green chilies
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup green enchilada sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant and slightly softened.
  4. Add the shredded chicken, green chilies, sour cream, and half of the chopped cilantro to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well to combine.
  5. Warm the corn tortillas in the microwave or on a comal (griddle) until pliable.
  6. Place a spoonful of the chicken mixture onto each tortilla, roll it up, and place seam-side down in a greased baking dish.
  7. In a saucepan, heat the chicken broth and green enchilada sauce over medium heat until simmering.
  8. Pour the sauce over the rolled enchiladas in the baking dish.
  9. Sprinkle the shredded Monterey Jack cheese evenly over the enchiladas.
  10. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Garnish with the remaining chopped cilantro.
  13. Serve hot and enjoy!
